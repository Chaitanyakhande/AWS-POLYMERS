About AVS Polymers

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by Mr. Anil Narayan Lokare, AVS Polymers is a leading manufacturer, exporter, and supplier of high-quality copper and aluminium busbars, copper jumpers, epoxy resin cast components, and insulators. We serve clients across the transformer, panel, furnace, and welding industries with precision-engineered products designed for performance and durability.

Our Mission

To deliver world-class electrical components and transformer parts that combine precision engineering, durability, and innovation, enhancing efficiency and safety in power distribution worldwide.

Our Core Values

Innovation

Adopting new technology for better solutions.

Customer Focus

Tailored products and dedicated service.

Integrity

Honest, transparent, and ethical in all dealings.

Our Sister Concern: Tejal Engineers

Tejal Engineers, located in Bhandup, Mumbai, is the sister concern of AVS POLYMERS. They focus on the manufacture of vital copper and brass components used in transformers and electrical equipment, ensuring conductivity and thermal performance.
